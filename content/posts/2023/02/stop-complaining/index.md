> All that happens is either a bearable or an unbearable event.
> If it is bearable, bear it. Stop complaining.
> If it is unbearable, stop complaining too. Your end means its end too.
> Remember, you can bear all that your mind declares bearable, by taking it as in your interest or duty.
>
> <cite> Marcus Aurelius - Meditations 10.3 </cite>
